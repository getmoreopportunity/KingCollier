# Outcome Journal - Decisions & Results

**Purpose:** Track important decisions and their outcomes. Learn what works.

---

## How to Use

After making a significant decision or trying a new approach:
1. Log it here immediately
2. Track results over time
3. Weekly review: what worked? what didn't?
4. Apply learnings to future decisions

---

## Template

```markdown
### [YYYY-MM-DD] - [Decision/Approach Title]

**Context:** [What led to this decision]
**Decision:** [What you decided to do]
**Expected Outcome:** [What you thought would happen]
**Actual Outcome:** [What actually happened]
**Learning:** [What you learned from this]
**Apply To:** [Future situations where this applies]
```
